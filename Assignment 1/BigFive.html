Effects Within A Team

In a team environment, I tend to be hard working and confident in my abilities. I can express my ideas to the group and argue my point, but I am also open to different perspectives. Whilst I appreciate constructive criticism and I am good at working together with others I do much better work in a collaborative team than a competitive environment. My strengths lie in working with others to achieve a common goal, as opposed to competing against others to come up with something better than them. This is something that has been reflected in this personality test, with a relatively high level of self consciousness reported.

Team Formation

In forming a team, the results of this test reflect the Myers-Briggs test closely. Clearly defined roles within a team will allow me to work at my best, with everyone able to work on their own area of responsibility and a fair distribution of work. I also value regular and open communication, so establishing regular meetings and other forms of communication are important. A collaborative team of like minded people would be advantageous as everyone should be willing to work hard on their own area, whilst giving honest and helpful feedback to others.
