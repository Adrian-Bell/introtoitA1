Overview

41% Kinesthetic / 32% Visual / 27% Auditory

My learning test results are balanced somewhat equally between kinesthetic, visual and auditory styles, but with a bias towards kinesthetic learning aptitude. This indicates that I learn best by trying things out myself and learning as I go. I feel that this is an accurate summary of my learning style, although if I am struggling with a particular aspect I will generally do more background research or seek help.

Effects Within A Team

In a team environment, this means that I do well when we start to work with a task quickly, and take a collaborative approach to troubleshooting and developing a concept as we move forward. I consider the result to reflect my proactive approach, and in a team environment this often means coming up with a concept which we are working towards, producing a plan to get there and workshopping issues that arise along the way.

Team Formation

When forming a team, I will perform best if the rest of the team has a similar attitude towards the creative process. I may become frustrated if we engage in a lot of background research before we start to work on the project so team members who are willing to jump in and start, but are open to changes as the project progresses would be ideal.
