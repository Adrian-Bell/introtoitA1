    Interest In IT

My interest in computers is centred on how they can be used as a tool to increase efficiency and make our lives easier. I would like to progress towards a role as a systems administrator or systems analyst, however as I learn more about the world of IT I am open to pursuing alternative paths as my interests and abilities become more advanced.

    Why RMIT?

    My aim in studying at RMIT is to get a bachelor's degree in Information Technology. In doing so I hope to
